About Our Silver Rings

Sterling silver — 92.5% pure silver alloyed with 7.5% copper for strength — has been a beloved metal in Indian jewelry for generations. At Anello, our silver ring collection celebrates this heritage with modern designs that suit daily wear, gifting, and special occasions without breaking the bank.

What Makes Anello Silver Rings Special?

Hallmarked 925 Sterling Silver

Every silver ring in our collection is made from 925 sterling silver — the internationally recognised standard for fine silver jewelry. Our silver is hallmarked and independently verified for purity.

Natural Gemstone Settings

Where other brands use synthetic stones, Anello uses natural gemstones — amethyst, moonstone, turquoise, labradorite, and more. Each stone is hand-selected for colour consistency and clarity, then individually set by skilled artisans.

Anti-Tarnish Rhodium Finish

Most of our silver rings receive a thin rhodium plating — the same bright metal used to finish white gold and platinum. This dramatically slows tarnishing, keeping your ring brilliant for years with minimal maintenance.

Popular Silver Ring Styles

Natural Gemstone Silver Rings

Our bestselling category: delicate silver rings featuring genuine semi-precious stones. From the deep violet of amethyst to the ethereal glow of labradorite, these rings make spectacular gifts and everyday statement pieces.

Silver Ring Care Guide

Silver requires a little more care than gold or platinum:

- Remove before bathing to prevent tarnish from water minerals

- Store in airtight zip-lock bags when not in use

- Polish with a soft silver polishing cloth

- Avoid contact with perfume, hairspray, and cleaning chemicals
