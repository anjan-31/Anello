Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all Anello rings BIS Hallmarked?+

Yes. Every gold and silver ring at Anello carries the BIS Hallmark — the Bureau of Indian Standards certification of metal purity. Our gold is independently tested and stamped before it leaves our workshop.

Do you offer free shipping across India?+

Yes. We offer complimentary Pan-India shipping on all orders, including to all 28 states and 8 union territories. No minimum order value.

What is your return and exchange policy?+

We offer a 15-day hassle-free return and exchange policy. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, return it in original condition for a full refund or exchange.

Are the diamonds certified?+

Yes. All diamond rings at Anello come with IGI or GIA certification verifying the Cut, Color, Clarity, and Carat weight of every diamond.

Do you offer EMI on purchases?+

Yes. We offer 0% EMI through major Indian credit cards and fintech partners including Bajaj Finserv, HDFC, and ICICI Bank on orders above ₹10,000.

How do I find my ring size?+

Visit our Ring Size Guide for a complete measurement method and Indian size chart. You can also contact our team via WhatsApp for personalised guidance.
